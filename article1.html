Robotics Season Begins

Date: October 6, 2024

The Stratford Robotics team is excited to announce the beginning of the 2024-2025 robotics season. This year, we are looking forward to participating in the FTC and FRC Competitions. The team has been working hard to prepare for the upcoming challenges and is eager to showcase their skills and creativity. Stay tuned for updates on our progress and competition results. Go Stratford Robotics!
