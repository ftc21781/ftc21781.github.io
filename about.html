About Us

Welcome to Stratford Preparatory Robotics Website

We are a passionate group of students dedicated to exploring the world of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) through hands-on robotics challenges. Competing in the FIRST Tech Challenge and the FIRST Robotics Competition, our team works collaboratively to design, build, and program robots that tackle real-world problems in creative ways.

Our mission is to inspire young minds to pursue careers in STEM fields by providing a fun and engaging environment where they can develop their skills and knowledge. We believe in the power of teamwork, creativity, and perseverance, and we strive to make a positive impact in our community.

Join us on our journey as we push the boundaries of innovation and excellence in robotics!
